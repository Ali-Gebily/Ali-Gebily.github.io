Welcome to Detect IT: Fish

The power of big data can protect our oceans and dramatically improve lives in areas supported by the ocean. Detect IT: Fish is a web-based tool that identifies potential flows of illegal trade in seafood based on discrepancies in reported import and export data between countries.

About

Developed by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) and TRAFFIC and powered by HPE Vertica technology, Detect IT: Fish enables automated collection, comparison, and analysis of customs data to help detect illegal trade in seafood at a global scale.

By ending illegal fishing, together we will create economic opportunity for local fishermen, ensure vital access to nutritious seafood, and provide a sustainable foundation for future generations.

Who should use
this tool?

Fisheries, customs and other government officials, seafood businesses, non-governmental organizations, academic institutions, and others working to prevent illegal fishing.

What does it do?

Detect IT: Fish provides an automated comparison of fisheries trade data between countries, and identifies discrepancies in reported import and export data to highlight potential trade in illegal products.

Where can it
be used?

Stakeholders anywhere in the world can investigate trade flows for hundreds of fishery products in reported trade from over 170 countries.
