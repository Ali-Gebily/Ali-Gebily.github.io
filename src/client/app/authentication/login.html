Welcome to Detect IT: Fish

The power of big data can protect our oceans and dramatically improve lives in areas supported by the ocean.
Detect IT: Fish is a web-based tool that identifies potential flows of illegal trade in seafood based on
discrepancies in reported import and export data between countries.
